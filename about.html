About Muribro

Filipino owned · Abu Dhabi, UAE · Serving the community with heart

Who We Are

Muribro Cars Repair Garage is a proud Filipino-owned automotive repair shop based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. We bring Filipino values — hard work, honesty, and genuine care — to every vehicle that comes through our doors.

Our tagline says it all: 'We put Care into Car Repair.' It's not just a slogan — it's how we operate every single day. We treat your car as if it were our own.

Our Specialization

We specialize in Japanese, Korean, and American car models. From Toyotas and Hondas to Kias and Hyundais, to Fords and Chevrolets — our mechanics are trained and experienced across all these platforms.

Our Values

We believe in transparency, fairness, and quality. You'll always know what we're working on, why, and what it costs — before we begin.

Community Roots

As part of the Filipino community in the UAE, we're proud to serve Abu Dhabi residents and expats alike. We've built lasting relationships with our customers based on trust and consistent results.
